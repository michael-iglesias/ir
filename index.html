Latest Project

Let Us Dorm is a tech startup we here at Open Momento have started ourselves. All three of us are college students who all ran into the same problem when we first moved up to FSU. We got stuck with an empty messy dorm room. So we came up with the idea of designing a site where future roomies can get together to purchase important household items and split the cost. We also incorporated a Live 'Group Shopping' feature which allows all roommates to video chat while shopping at our ecommerce store. All new users are able to register using their Facebook or Twitter accounts and our shopping cart is powered by PayPal putting all of our customers at ease while completing a transaction on the site.
